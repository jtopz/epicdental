Frequently Asked Questions

Quickly find answers to your membership questions.

Epic Dental Plan is NOT INSURANCE, but rather a licensed Dental Savings Plan that's offered through your local Perfect Dental office. This means that Perfect Dental offices have agreed to charge their plan members at a discounted price. Unlike dental insurance, Dental Savings Plans have no waiting periods, no pre-existing conditions, no maximums, no exclusions, and no hassles.

Epic Dental Plan members are charged based on a transparent, pre-negotiated member-only fee schedule. All preventive, basic, major, cosmetic, and orthodontic procedures are discounted for active plan members only. Plan members receive 20-50% savings on all preventive, basic, major, cosmetic and orthodontic dental care at their local Perfect Dental office. Plan members simply pay the discounted amount at the time of service.

Becoming a member with Epic Dental Plan is very inexpensive. Members are billed annually and memberships are only $70/year for an Individual Plan, $90/year for Two Individuals Plan, and $120/year for a Family Plan.

Taking proper care of your oral health is extremely important. Americans are having significant problems with affordability and access to quality dentistry. Dental insurance doesn't make sense for everyone, as there are waiting periods, maximums, and exclusions. This has created an environment where many Americans don't have dental insurance and are paying out of pocket at their dental provider. Epic Dental Plan addresses these issues by providing a real solution for Americans who need quality dental care at affordable, transparent prices.

Epic Dental Plan differentiates itself from other Dental Savings Plans by including a $1,000/year warranty coverage on dental work rendered to plan members by their affiliated provider. There is no additional cost associated with Keep On Smilin' Assurance coverage. Plan members must continue to visit their affiliated provider for regularly scheduled visits and must be current with their annual membership when services are rendered and at the time the unforeseen dental issue occurs. *Terms & Conditions Apply

Epic Dental Plan has no waiting periods, exclusions or maximums. This means you can use your plan immediately after you enroll and as frequently as needed. If you're enrolled in a Perfect Dental office or online you would give your affiliated Perfect Dental office your name so they can verify eligibility while you're in the office.

Epic Dental Plan can be used at all Perfect Dental locations across Massachusetts and New Hampshire. A list of Perfect Dental locations can be accessed via the plan website or by calling (888) 771-4942 Monday - Friday from 9am - 7pm EST.

Epic Dental Plan carries a 1-year membership term. The annual term lasts 365 days from enrollment date. All plans are automatically renewed unless a cancellation request is sent by the member to the plan administrators. All active members receive direct mail, email, and phone call renewal notifications prior to their annual renewal date so there are no surprises.

Epic Dental Plan is NOT INSURANCE, but rather a licensed dental savings plan offered through your local dental office. Members in good standing with their annual membership fee are eligible to receive transparent, member-only discounts from the normal retail fees that participating offices typically charge self-pay patients for dental services rendered. Plan details and member savings are exclusive to participating offices and may vary by location. Membersy does not make payments directly to dental providers for services rendered to plan members. Members are obligated to pay for all dental services, but will receive a discount on services rendered by participating dental providers. The plan is not a qualified health plan under the Affordable Health Act. The plan does not meet the minimum creditable coverage requirements under MGLC.111M and 956 CMR 5.00.
